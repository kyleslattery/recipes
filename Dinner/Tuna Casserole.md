* 1/3 - 1/2 Casserole noodles
* 2 regular cans tuna
* milk
* cream of mushroom soup
* mexican or cheddar cheese
* breadcrumbs

1. Boil noodles for half time
2. Mix tuna, 1/2 can milk, and soup
3. Mix in noodles
4. Spread in dish
5. Add cheese
6. Add breadcrumbs (optional: melt butter with breadcrumbs)
7. Bake 20 min @ 350
8. Broil at end
